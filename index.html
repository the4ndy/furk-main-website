Furk is freedom transparancy security


Furk is an indescribable collective of miscreants banded together to do nothing . Forged from laziness and bound by Occam's Razor eternally in search of semi-wholesome fun .

Open Source Freedom

Everything created under the Furk monikor will always and forever be free as in speech. All Furk Projects fall under the FURK license unless otherwise stated.

Too Hold to Cold

Furk strives to implement the most bleeding edge, alpha / beta software available. If you want to see non-production software implemented in production enviornments, look no further.

Best Effort Security

The only truely secure computer is powered off, encased in cement, and resting at the bottom of the Hudson river. With Furk, security will always at least be considered and made openly transparant.

FurkChat
